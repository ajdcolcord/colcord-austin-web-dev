Prepper

The Complete Prep Station

Whether you need to store your favorite recipes, organize tasks for your restaurant's prep staff, or set a timer for something in the oven, Prepper is here for you!

Created with the restaurant in mind, Prepper is a unique web application that keeps the focus on what the cooks are working on. Collaborate and organize more effectively with your staff through an easy to use interface that allows quick organization of tasks.

Prepper works well and looks beautiful on any smartphone, tablet, or desktop and is available from anywhere.

Recipe Book

Store and manage all of the restaurant's recipes in one convenient place. See which recipes you have added, worked on, or modified within the last 5 days along with the list of all of your recipes. Use powerful tools to start a timer while you're viewing a recipe, add it to the prep ToDo list, and even see ingredient measurements with a built-in portion multiplier.

Prep List

Be more collaborative with your kitchen staff, through an engaging view of the tasks that need to be prepared. Organized clearly into three sections - ToDo, In Progress, and Completed - all employees can see what is going on in the kitchen, and keep track of who is working on what.

Timers

Enjoy the flexibility of creating multiple timers in order to keep track of what you are cooking. Through the employee view, see a personalized set of timers created only by you. As a restaurant manager, you can see all ongoing timers from all of your employees in an instant.
