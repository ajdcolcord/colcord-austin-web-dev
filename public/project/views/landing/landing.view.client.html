Prepper

The Complete Prep Station

Whether you need to store your favorite recipes, organize your tasks for your restaurant's prep staff, or set timers for all of the different items you are cooking, Prepper is here for you!

Created with the restaurant in mind, Prepper is a unique web application that keeps the focus on what the cooks are working on. Collaborate and organize more effectively with your staff through an easy to use interface that allows reorganizing of tasks in place.

Prepper works well and looks beautiful on any smartphone, tablet, or desktop, available from anywhere.
