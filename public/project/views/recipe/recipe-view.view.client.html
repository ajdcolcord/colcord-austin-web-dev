Chicken Panini

Prep Time (min): 10

Type: Sandwich

A warm chicken panini with cheddar cheese with avocado on ciabatta bread

Ingredients
Ingredient Amount Style
Chicken Breast 1 Diced
Cheddar Cheese 1 Sliced
Avocado 1/4 Sliced
Ciabatta Bread 1
Directions
  1. Cook chicken breast fully
  2. Steam cooked chicken breast for 2 minutes
  3. Cut chicken breast in half
  4. Place Cheddar Cheese, chicken breast, and avocado in Ciabatta
  5. Panini press for 2 minutes
